## Initials avatar logic

When `avatarUrl` is absent, render a circular div with initials derived from `name`:
- Take first letter of first name + first letter of last name (if present)
- Background: `bg-[#2a2a2a]` with `border border-white/10`
- Text: `font-mono text-sm text-[#a3a3a3]`
- Size: `w-10 h-10 rounded-full flex items-center justify-center shrink-0`
